Project KryptosAbyss

Introducing equality to cultural accessibility.

Okay so... what is this?

Honestly this started as me getting frustrated. I wanted to watch one movie last month and ended up needing 3 different subscriptions. By the time I found it, I lost interest. That happens way too often now.

KryptosAbyss is my attempt at building something that helps people actually Get stuff — movies, books, articles, learning material, music and many more — without bouncing between 10 platforms and paying them separately.

A quick history of the internet (because context matters)

The internet has been around since '69, but it didn't really hit normal people until the mid-90s. Here's the rough growth:

1969 it begins (mostly military / academic)
1995 ~16 million people online
2000 361 million — about 6% of humans
2015 hit 3 billion users
2025 ~5.6 billion. Roughly 2 out of 3 people.

Point is — most of the world is online now. But access to information, entertainment, knowledge and culture is getting harder, not easier.

The monopoly problem

It used to be: pay one subscription, watch everything on that service. Now? You pay the subscription and rent the new movie on top of it. Plus a separate app for sports. Plus another for that one show your friend keeps talking about.

The issues are the affordability, many apps, many expensive subscriptions, many passwords, region locks, expired free trials u forgot to cancel and many more .

Here's a rough breakdown of what monthly "legal access" or "unpirated acess" actually costs an average person:

What you pay for Cost Notes
OTT subscription $12 just to get in the door
Rental for a new movie $6 yes, even after subscribing
One e-book $15 academic ones cost more
News paywall $5 for one publication
Total $38 per month
What most students can spend universally ~$15 being generous
The gap $23 and this is why piracy exists

(numbers vary by time,region,need and other factors, but the pattern is the almost same everywhere)

Problem With Piracy

Piracy nowdays is almost full of problems. Most website are filled with malware, virus and other hazardous programm. They evntually harm your pc or just destruct it.

Those numerous redirects, 18+ advertisments, malicious urls, phising links and many more are anohter bad dimensions of piracy.

So what's the idea

KryptosAbyss isn't trying to be a "piracy" service. There's enough of those. What I'm trying to build is more of a new platform — a place where you search for something and you get stream, download or view link immediately.

A few things I'm focused on:

  • One platform for almost alll needs No bouncing from website to website or app to app needed.
  • Safer than the alternatives. Sketchy sites carry malware. I want to point people toward real and verified sources.
  • Cultural focus. Allowing people to take part in making and evolution of culture.
"Access to culture should depend on curiosity, not on how many subscriptions you can afford."

That's basically the whole thing. It's early. There's a lot to figure out. But I think it's worth trying.
